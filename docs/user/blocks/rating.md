# Rating Block

The rating block enables participants to provide star ratings for feedback or assessment. Use this to gather feedback about experiences, difficulty, or content quality.

![](/static/images/docs/user/blocks/block-rating.webp)

## Configuration

- **Prompt**: The question or statement participants are rating
- **Max Rating**: Configure the rating scale (3-10 stars, default: 5)
- **Points**: Award points when participants submit their rating (all ratings award equal points)

## Notes

- Ratings cannot be changed once submitted
- Ideal for finish pages to gather post-game feedback
